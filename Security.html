Safety & security

Get help quickly, share your location only with people you trust, choose safer routes around campus, and report incidents so the university can act. This page is a prototype—connect buttons and forms to your backend when you build the full app.

Emergency SOS

Press the button to alert campus security and your selected emergency contacts. In a live app this could use a long-press or countdown to reduce accidental triggers.

Demo only: no alert is sent. A full version would dial security and notify your chosen contacts by SMS or in-app message.

Will notify: Campus security + contacts you set in Profile (e.g. trusted friends or family).

Live location sharing

Share your live location with selected contacts for a limited time—useful when walking late or meeting someone. You stay in control and can stop sharing at any time.

Sharing with (selected contacts)

  • Mum
  • Alex (flatmate)
  • SU buddy scheme

In the full app you would choose contacts in Settings and set how long sharing lasts (e.g. 30 minutes or until you arrive).

Safe route navigation

Plan a walking route that avoids areas reported as poorly lit, isolated, or higher risk. Data improves when students submit incident reports (see below).

Incident report

Report harassment, unsafe areas, broken lighting, or anything else that affects safety on campus. Reports can help the university prioritise repairs and patrols.
