SYNK- Smart Campus

University life, made smarter and safer for women in South Africa

SYNK brings practical tools, discreet support, and an AI assistant together in one place—so students can focus on studies and community safely across South African campuses.

What is SYNK?

A web application designed for students and staff at South African universities: period support, safety features, campus navigation, timetabling, health check-ins, peer community, mentorship—and a chatbot assistant to help you find what you need quickly.

About the web app

SYNK is an AI-powered companion for day-to-day university life, centered on women’s safety and wellbeing. It connects users to campus facilities, helps stay organised, and offers safer navigation across South African campuses.

Features

Overview of what SYNK offers. Each module focuses on student safety, accessibility, wellbeing, and community.

Period Support

  • Request pads or other essentials discreetly through campus distribution points.
  • Optional reminders and self-care tips during your cycle.

Safety & Security

  • Emergency SOS: alert campus security and trusted contacts.
  • Live location sharing: share location with selected contacts.
  • Safe route navigation: avoid unsafe or poorly lit areas.
  • Incident reporting: report harassment, broken lights, or unsafe spots anonymously.

Smart Campus Life

  • Class schedule organiser: timetables, assignment deadlines, calendar view.
  • Room & facility finder: lecture halls, study spaces, accessible toilets.
  • Accessibility support: request assistance and view accessible routes.

Health & Wellbeing

  • Quick access to period products on campus.
  • Mental health check-ins: daily mood tracker and links to counselling services.

Community & Support

  • Women’s forum: share advice, events, and local tips.
  • Mentorship & tutoring: connect with peers or academic mentors.

AI Assistant & Accounts

  • Chatbot: get help finding services and features.
  • Secure login & registration: optional anonymous identity for sensitive actions.

Anonymous Identity

Use Smart Campus SA at the level you are comfortable with. Features like incident reporting or forum posts can be pseudonymous. Sensitive functions (SOS or mentorship) require verified accounts for effective response.
